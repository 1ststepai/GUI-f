## Retention Peaks Identified

### Peak 1 (Opening Hook - First 30 seconds)
**The Pattern Interrupt:** "AI just changed forever. And if you're still using ChatGPT like a fancy Google search, you're about to get left behind."

**Why it works:** Contrarian statement that challenges the viewer's current behavior. Creates immediate cognitive dissonance.

### Peak 2 (The Revelation - ~2-3 min)
**The Core Insight:** "Most people are still chatting with AI, asking it questions, getting answers, maybe writing some emails. But the people who are going to win the next decade, they're not chatting with AI anymore. They're putting AI to work. AI agents that actually do things, build things, complete entire projects while the human sleeps."

**Why it works:** Separates the "chatters" from the "builders." Appeals to ambition and fear of missing out.

### Peak 3 (The Action Step - ~8-9 min)
**The Call to Action:** "Pick one tool based on what I said earlier. Number two, pick one task that you're doing every week that takes you a lot of time. Three, have the agent do it for you today."

**Why it works:** Simple 3-step framework that makes it impossible to not take action.

## Adapted Scripts

### LONG-FORM SCRIPT (45 seconds)

**[HOOK - 0:05]**
If you're still using ChatGPT like a fancy Google search, this is your wake-up call.

**[BODY - 0:25]**
There's a new category of AI that's different from everything you've used. It's not a chatbot. It's a worker. While you sleep, it builds your campaigns, analyzes your data, handles your repetitive tasks. The people winning right now aren't chatting with AI. They're hiring it.

**[CALL TO ACTION - 0:15]**
Here's what to do right now. One - pick one tool. Two - pick one task that eats your time every week. Three - give that task to your agent today. Do this and you'll understand why this is bigger than the internet ever was.
